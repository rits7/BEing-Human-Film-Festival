Guidelines

You must be an enrolled High School Student to participate and may require parental/Guardian permission to register.

Registration and Entry Guidelines
  • Registration is currently free.
  • Registration and submission of your film must be completed by the advertised closing date.
  • Your film must be no longer than 5 minutes (including titles and credits)
  • Your film must in some way depict stories, actions or situations that communicate compassion, understanding and insight into what we share as human beings. It is recommended that little or no language is used. Where possible tell your story visually.
  • You need to have read and understood all the terms and conditions. Please contact us if you have any questions. Submission of your film is an indication to us that you have signed off on all the terms and conditions.

Submitting Your Entry
  • All entries have to be submitted online
  • Upload your film to Youtube or Vimeo
  • Complete the registration form and submit, this will include a link to your film
  • Your entry will not be accepted if:
    • You have not completed and submitted the registration form.
    • Your film is over the 5 minute time limit (including titles and credits)
    • Your entry is not in by midnight on the closing date.
    • Your entry is in violation of any of the terms and conditions with particular reference to issues of copyright on all aspects of the production.

Recommended Specifications
  • Please upload your film to YouTube or Vimeo in the highest quality possible
  • Video Format: H264, MPEG-2 or MPEG-4 preferred
  • Aspect Ratio: 16:9 (letterboxed if necessary)
  • Resolution: 1920x1080 preferred
  • Audio Format: MP3 or AAC preferred
  • Frames per second: 25
  • Maximum file size: 1.8 GB

Be Copyright Aware

Ensure you have not breached any copyright. The best way to do this to create everything yourself including music and sound effects.

Take a look at youtube copyright pages for more details information. If you have any concerns about copyright it is important to check you have not breached anyone's rights.
